chasing a little past_ostrale

An installation exploring memory and place, presented at OSTRALE in Dresden, Germany. The work creates a spatial narrative where fragments of memories are captured and displayed, inviting viewers to reflect on the ephemeral nature of recollection. Through the interplay of light, shadow, and projection, the installation creates a dreamlike environment where past and present converge, suggesting that our memories are constantly being reconstructed and reinterpreted.
