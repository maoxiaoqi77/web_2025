Exploring the emotional connection between humans and animals through a dog's perspective. The installation incorporates elements of daily life, presenting a unique observational viewpoint of life. This work invites viewers to experience the world from a canine perspective, challenging our assumptions about consciousness, loyalty, and the simple joys found in everyday moments. Through carefully arranged domestic objects and interactive elements, the installation creates an intimate space for reflection on our relationships with our animal companions.
