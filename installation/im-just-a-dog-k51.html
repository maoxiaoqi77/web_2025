I'M JUST A DOG_k51

An installation exploring the relationship between humans and dogs, presented at K51 Gallery. The work creates a spatial narrative that invites viewers to reflect on the unique bond between humans and their canine companions. Through the interplay of light, shadow, and projection, the installation creates a dreamlike environment where the boundaries between human and animal perspectives blur, suggesting a deeper connection between species.
