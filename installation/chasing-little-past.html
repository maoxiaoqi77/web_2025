An immersive installation that explores the ephemeral nature of memories and time. Through a carefully orchestrated interplay of light, shadow, and space, this work invites viewers to traverse the delicate boundary between past and present. The installation creates a contemplative environment where fragments of memories materialize and dissolve, encouraging reflection on how we perceive and preserve our personal histories. Each element serves as a metaphorical thread, weaving together moments that exist between remembrance and forgetting.
