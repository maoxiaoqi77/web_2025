Using water as a medium to explore the fluidity of life and time. Through the flow and stagnation of water, it presents a poetic visual experience, revealing the balance between stillness and motion in life. This work transforms the gallery space into a contemplative environment where water becomes both subject and medium, inviting viewers to reflect on the temporal nature of existence and the delicate equilibrium between movement and rest.
