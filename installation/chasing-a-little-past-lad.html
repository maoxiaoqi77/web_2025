chasing a little past_LAD

An installation artwork about chasing fragments of memories, presented at LAD Gallery. Through the interweaving of light and shadow, it reveals forgotten moments in the flow of time, guiding viewers to reconsider the connection between past and present. This work explores the ephemeral nature of memory and how we constantly try to grasp fleeting moments from our past. The installation creates a space where visitors can experience the sensation of memories slipping through their fingers, just as they attempt to hold onto them.
