never talk in the garden_TWS_HONGO

"Never talk in the garden" is an installation artwork that explores the silent communication between humans and nature. Through visual and spatial elements, it creates an environment where viewers can experience the unspoken dialogue that exists in garden spaces. This installation was exhibited at TOKYO WONDER SITE, Tokyo, Japan in 2015.
